Why ZK-Solvency?

The digital asset industry suffers from a lack of transparency. Traditional audits are slow, expensive, and rely on trusting human auditors who only look at snapshots in time.

ZK-Solvency revolutionizes trust by enabling continuous, cryptographically-proven audits of institutional reserves and liabilities. We utilize zero-knowledge proofs to allow exchanges and custodians to mathematically guarantee their solvency without revealing sensitive commercial data or user balances.

How ZK Audits Work

A completely trustless pipeline from institutional wallets to public on-chain verification.

01

Data Ingestion

The institution's entire liability set and asset lists are securely ingested into the ZK prover circuit locally.

02

Circuit Generation

A zk-SNARK proof is generated verifying Assets > Liabilities, culminating in an unforgeable Merkle Root.

03

On-Chain Publication

The mathematical proof and root hash are securely published on-chain, allowing anyone to verify status.
